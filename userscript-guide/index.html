NexDirect - Userscript Guide

Step 1

Register the NexDirect URI scheme for your user account through the Settings menu (click the logo in the bottom right). [I know this sounds like technical dribble, but essentially it allows your browser to communicate with NexDirect.]

Step 2

Ensure you have your favorite userscript manager installed in your browser. (Tested with Tampermonkey for Google Chrome, Greasemonkey on Firefox should work too.)
